📷 Photo Guidelines

For an accurate home insurance quote, please include:

  • All 4 sides of your home - Front, back, left, and right views
  • Roof angle - Best visible angle showing roof condition
  • Any detached structures - Garage, shed, pool house, etc.
  • Special features - Pool, deck, hot tub, solar panels, etc.

💡 Tip: Stand back to capture the entire structure in each photo

To add coverage for new additions/improvements:

  • New addition/improvement - Multiple angles
  • Overall home view - Showing new addition in context
  • Close-up details - Materials, fixtures, special features
  • Receipts/invoices - If available for valuation

For claim documentation, please include:

  • All damaged areas - Wide shots and close-ups
  • Cause of damage - If visible (tree, water source, etc.)
  • Affected rooms/areas - Multiple angles
  • Damaged personal property - Individual items
  • Overall context - Showing extent of damage

⚠️ Document everything before making repairs if safe to do so

For policy review, please include:

  • Current renewal notice - All pages
  • Declaration page - From current policy
  • Any changes to property - New additions, renovations
  • Updated home photos - If significant changes made

For an accurate auto insurance quote, please include:

  • All 4 sides of vehicle - Front, back, both sides
  • VIN number - Dashboard or driver door frame
  • Odometer reading - Current mileage
  • License plates - Front and back if applicable
  • Any existing damage - Document pre-existing issues

💡 Tip: Clean vehicle and take photos in good lighting

To add a vehicle to your policy:

  • All 4 sides of new vehicle - Complete views
  • VIN number - Clear photo
  • Purchase paperwork - Bill of sale or dealer invoice
  • Title or registration - If available
  • Odometer reading - Current mileage

For auto claim documentation:

  • All damage areas - Multiple angles and close-ups
  • Impact points - Where contact occurred
  • Interior damage - If applicable
  • Other vehicle(s) - If multi-vehicle accident
  • Scene photos - Location, road conditions if relevant
  • Police report number - Photo of exchange slip or report

⚠️ Take photos before moving vehicle if safe to do so

For auto policy review, please include:

  • Current renewal notice - All pages
  • Declaration page - From current policy
  • Current registration - For all vehicles
  • Driver's licenses - All household drivers
  • Updated vehicle photos - If changes/modifications made
